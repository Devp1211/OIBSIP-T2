Remembering Freddie Mercury

"The most important thing is to live a fabulous life. As long as it's fabulous I don't care how long it is.

Freddie Mercury was a British singer and songwriter who achieved worldwide fame as the lead vocalist and pianist of the rock band Queen. His charismatic stage performances often saw him interact with the audience, as displayed at the 1985 Live Aid concert.

Mercury defied the conventions of a rock frontman with his theatrical style, influencing the artistic direction of Queen.

Mercury wrote numerous hits for Queen :

  • Bohemian Rhapsody
  • Radio Ga Ga
  • We Are the Champions
  • Crazy Little Thing Called Love
  • Killer Queen

His career with Queen was dramatised in the 2018 biopic Bohemian Rhapsody. We are reminded to pursue passion, challenge the norm, and embrace change. Freddie Mercury, your legacy lives on.
